Kitutu Chache North Constituency (NG-CDF)

About the Bursary Program

The Kitutu Chache North NG-CDF Bursary Program provides financial assistance to needy and deserving students pursuing secondary, tertiary, college and university education.

FY 2025 / 2026 Applications Open

Eligibility & Requirements

  • ✔ Must be a resident of Kitutu Chache North Constituency
  • ✔ Admission to a recognized institution
  • ✔ Completed bursary application form
  • ✔ Current fees structure
  • ✔ Copy of National ID / Birth Certificate
  • ✔ Parents/Guardian ID copies
  • ✔ Death certificate (for orphans)
  • ✔ Admission letter (tertiary students)
  • ✔ Student ID (college/university)
